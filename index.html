    Project Description

    Through a tentative design timeline,a mobile app design project will progress through the following stages:

    1.Customer Meetings/ Design Specifications: If possible, I will meet with you to discuss the specific features and purpose of your app.

    2.UX Flow Charts: I will create a flow chart visualizing how users interact with your app and its features.

    3.App Screen Wireframes: I will create basic sketches of the app’s various screens and menus.

    4.Initial Mockups: I will create detailed color mockups of the app’s various screens and menus.

    5.Interactive Prototype: I will create an interactive mockup that will mimic the UX of the final app to demonstrate how users will interact with the final version.
